Welcome to Sunday Hotel Frankfurt Airport

Perfect Location

Conveniently located in Neu-Isenburg, just 15 minutes by taxi from Frankfurt Airport and close to the A3, A5, and A661 motorways. Ideal for both business travellers and tourists exploring the Frankfurt Rhein-Main region.

Modern Comfort

Settle into our contemporary guest rooms offering approx. 20 m² of space with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TV, tea & coffee making facilities, and individual climate control.
