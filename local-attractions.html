Perfectly Placed for Business & Leisure

Neu-Isenburg is located only 13 minutes from Frankfurt Airport and 12 minutes from the Stadium of Eintracht Frankfurt and the city centre, making Sunday Hotel Frankfurt Airport ideal for leisure guests as well as business travellers. Discover Frankfurt’s historic landmarks, museums, shopping areas, parks and nearby regional highlights.

Things To Do

Discover Frankfurt & Beyond

Römer

Frankfurt’s historic square and one of the city’s most recognisable landmarks.

Museumsufer

Frankfurt’s famous museum district along the river Main, ideal for art, culture and history lovers.

Frankfurt City Centre

Discover cosmopolitan shops, restaurants, skyline views and the energy of central Frankfurt.

Main Tower

Enjoy panoramic views from one of Frankfurt’s most famous skyscrapers.

Palmengarten

A peaceful botanical garden with beautiful landscapes, greenhouses and seasonal displays.

Alte Oper Frankfurt

Frankfurt’s elegant concert hall and a cultural highlight in the city centre.

Goethe House

Visit the birthplace of Johann Wolfgang von Goethe and explore Frankfurt’s literary heritage.

Frankfurt Airport

One of Europe’s major international airports, easily accessible from the hotel.

Mainz

Discover Mainz with its historic old town, cathedral and Gutenberg Museum.

Wiesbaden

Visit Wiesbaden, famous for its elegant architecture, thermal springs and casino.

Heidelberg

A romantic historic city with a famous castle, old town and riverside scenery.

Golf Club Neuhof e.V.

A popular nearby golf destination for guests looking to relax outdoors.
